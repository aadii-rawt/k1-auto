Application Instructions

  1. Open Chrome with Remote Debugging.
  2. Login to Website GetID admin panel on 1st tab
  3. Login to Website K1 on 2nd tab
  4. Login both Websites
  5. Open the Withdraw request on GetID and litst of beneficiary on k1
  6. Now Enter your MPIN and minimum and maximum withdraw amount
  7. Click Start Automation.
  8. Monitor the logs.
  9. Click Stop whenever required.
